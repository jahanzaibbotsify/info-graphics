Pros and Cons of Online Learning

Infographic

Benefits to Online Learning

  • Possibility of reaching out to a greater number of students

  • Flexible schedules

  • Classes are recorded automatically for posterity

  • Teaching aid tools make it convenient for students and teachers

  • Class be taken anywhere

Downsides To Online Learning

  • Not the same as being in the classroom

  • Online tools are not always reliable

  • Hands on exercises are difficult to monitor

  • Online teaching is ineffective in teaching kindergarteners and toddlers

  • Flexible scheduling can be great for teachers but not for students
